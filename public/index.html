Connecting Indian Cinema

CineLink is India's professional networking platform for the film industry — connecting actors, directors, crew members, and cinema enthusiasts across Bollywood, Tollywood, Kollywood, and regional cinema.

Safety Policy

Child Safety Standards

CineLink has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content. We are committed to maintaining a safe platform for every user and to fully cooperating with law enforcement where violations occur.

Prohibited Content & Conduct

We do not permit, and will immediately remove, any account, content, or communication that:

  • Sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors in any way
  • Facilitates contact between minors and adults for inappropriate purposes
  • Shares, distributes, or solicits child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
  • Violates child safety laws in India or any other jurisdiction

Accounts found in violation are permanently banned and reported to the relevant authorities without prior notice.

How to Report

Users can report any safety concern directly within the app via the Report function on any profile, post, or message. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 48 hours. You may also email us directly at cinelink011@gmail.com.

Law Enforcement Cooperation

CineLink complies with all applicable child safety laws and cooperates fully with law enforcement and regulatory authorities. This includes reporting to:

  • India's National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB)
  • The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable
  • Any other competent authority under applicable law
